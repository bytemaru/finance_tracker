Upload Your ASB CSV Export

Please upload the CSV file you downloaded from ASB online banking. Expected columns: Date, Unique Id, Tran Type, Cheque Number, Payee, Memo, Amount.

What Happens Next?

  • Chunking of large files for efficient processing
  • Deduplication of records to avoid double-loading
  • Meta data storage in Redis
  • Export to data lake (HDFS) and analytics database
  • Access visualisatons in finance tracker portal

Visualisation Portal

    Once your CSV is processed, head over to the portal to explore an interactive treemap of your transaction history. Each rectangle represents a category (e.g. Groceries, Rent, Taxi), sized proportionally to your spending or income in that category.

  • Hover over any block to see exact totals and transaction counts.
  • Filter by date range to focus on specific months or years.

Instantly spot anomalies, optimize your budget, and make data-driven financial decisions. Transactions from ASB are processed in real time and rendered in clear, beautiful graphics.
